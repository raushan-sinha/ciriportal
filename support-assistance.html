Support Assistance Program

Our 24/7 Support Assistance Program is designed to ensure that all distributors and retailers receive rapid solutions to operational and technical issues. We provide:

  • ✅ A 24/7 ticketing system to log and monitor issue progress.
  • ✅ AI-powered chatbot for instant answers to common problems.
  • ✅ Human support escalation within minutes for complex queries.
  • ✅ Support across platforms including web, mobile, and email.

How It Works:

  1. Log in to your CiriPortal Admin dashboard.
  2. Click on “Help Desk” and input your name, email and phone.
  3. Choose your issue type and add a description.
  4. Submit your issue status in real-time.
  5. Use chatbot for instant answers before raising a ticket. This Chatbot is available on the Main Page
