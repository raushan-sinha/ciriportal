Loyalty & Reward Schemes

At CiriPortal, we believe in rewarding consistent commitment. Our Loyalty & Reward Schemes are designed to appreciate our loyal retailers and distributors through a structured point-based system.

🎯 How Points Are Earned:

  • ✅ Every product purchase earns points based on value.
  • ✅ Bonus points for bulk or recurring purchases.
  • ✅ Referral-based points for introducing new customers.
  • ✅ Additional rewards for early/on-time payments.

🏆 What You Get in Return:

  1. 🎁 Exclusive discounts on future orders.
  2. 💼 Free promotional materials and standees.
  3. 🎫 Vouchers and gift cards based on milestones.
  4. 🏅 Monthly leaderboard rankings and recognition.

Whether you’re a new retailer or a long-time distributor, CiriPortal’s reward system ensures that loyalty truly pays off.
