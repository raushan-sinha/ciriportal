Returns & Refunds

Eligibility for Return

You may request a return or replacement if:

  • You received a wrong product.
  • The item is damaged, defective, or expired.
  • The packaging is tampered or unsealed at delivery.
  • You are not satisfied with a personal-care item (unused & unopened only).
  • You’ve received expired or spoiled food/drink products.

Note: Digital or downloadable products (e.g., eBooks) are non-returnable.

Return Request Window

You must raise a return request within 7 days of receiving the order.
Beyond 7 days, returns/refunds are not applicable.

Refund Process

  • Once your return is approved and the item is picked up & verified, refunds are initiated.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 working days.
  • For COD orders, refunds are issued via bank transfer or CiriPortal Wallet.

Steps to Initiate Return

  1. Go to Order History in your dashboard.
  2. Click Return / Replace.
  3. Select the reason and upload a photo (if required).
  4. Our team reviews and updates you within 24 hours.

Exchange Policy

In some cases we send a replacement instead of a refund—subject to stock availability.

Important Notes

  • Items must be returned in their original packaging.
  • Opened food or personal-care items are non-returnable unless faulty.
  • Bulk or promotional orders may have separate return conditions.
