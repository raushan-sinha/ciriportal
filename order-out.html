Order In Out

The Order In Out section in CiriPortal allows distributors to track the complete movement of goods in and out of their inventory in real-time. This page displays all recent outgoing orders, enabling you to review delivery details, verify order accuracy, and ensure timely distribution to retailers and customers. The intuitive design ensures quick identification of orders by product type, customer, and delivery status, allowing for efficient operations management. Whether it’s bulk shipments to retailers or single deliveries to customers, you can monitor every stage of order fulfillment right from here. By providing a clear and organized view of your outwards orders, CiriPortal helps reduce errors, maintain transparency, and improve service reliability.
