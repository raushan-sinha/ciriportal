About CiriPortal

Whether it’s nourishing your family with safe and tasty food options, enhancing your daily routine with personal care essentials, or staying informed through engaging media and educational content – CiriPortal is your all-in-one destination.

In personal care, we promote wellness through nature-powered formulations. Through our education and media channels, we foster informed communities and empower the next generation. Join us on this journey towards healthier living, smarter learning, and better being – only at CiriPortal.

We believe in accessibility, inclusivity, and sustainability. Our offerings are tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals and families across India and beyond. From local flavors to global standards, we ensure that every product reflects our unwavering commitment to quality and care. Our platform is constantly evolving to stay in sync with modern trends and technologies, helping users find everything they need in one place. As we look to the future, CiriPortal aims to become not just a platform but a way of life — enriching communities and creating meaningful impact through innovation, service, and responsibility.

CiriPortal – Sustainability

At CiriPortal, sustainability is not just a commitment — it's a way of life. We believe in preserving nature while delivering high-quality products and services to our customers. Our practices are deeply rooted in environmental responsibility and social awareness.

We have taken bold steps towards a greener future by integrating eco-friendly initiatives into every aspect of our business. From sourcing raw materials to packaging and delivery, every step is reviewed through a sustainability lens.

Our Green Commitments

  • Green Packaging: We use biodegradable and recyclable packaging materials to reduce plastic waste and environmental impact.
  • Waste Management: Our facilities follow strict waste segregation, recycling, and disposal systems aligned with global best practices.
  • Energy Efficiency: Adoption of energy-efficient equipment and practices helps us conserve power in manufacturing and distribution units.
  • Water Conservation: Implementation of rainwater harvesting and water recycling systems across our operations.
  • Carbon Footprint Reduction: We optimize our logistics network to reduce CO2 emissions during transportation and delivery.
  • Awareness Programs: We conduct regular employee and community awareness campaigns about eco-conscious behavior and sustainable living.

By aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), CiriPortal is taking long-term steps to create a better planet for future generations. Join us in making every choice count — for you, for us, and for the Earth.
