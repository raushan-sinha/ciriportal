CiriPortal Distributor Details

Distributors are the heart of CiriPortal. Our platform provides each distributor with a dedicated dashboard where they can:

  • Upload and manage multiple product categories
  • Assign pricing based on location, retailer type, or volume
  • Track real-time order requests from registered retailers
  • Generate automatic sales reports and performance insights
  • Manage delivery and logistic partners
  • Offer seasonal promotions and discounts
  • Communicate directly with retailers through chat or announcements

Distributors also have access to service and support tools, which allow them to schedule training sessions, manage complaints, and ensure top-tier customer satisfaction.

CiriPortal Retailer Details

Retailers on CiriPortal gain access to an easy-to-use interface tailored to their business needs. Through their dashboard, retailers can:

  • Browse products from multiple verified distributors
  • Place bulk or item-wise orders with expected delivery timelines
  • Access real-time stock and price availability
  • Participate in loyalty and cashback programs
  • Track their order history, invoice status, and returns
  • Submit queries or requests directly to the distributor
  • Get updated about offers, schemes, and announcements

Retailers benefit from increased transparency, faster deliveries, and access to a wider range of products.
