About CiriPortal Services

CiriPortal is a cutting-edge B2B platform built to streamline and digitize the distribution and retail ecosystem. Our mission is to empower local businesses, distributors, and retailers by providing an intuitive and reliable solution that transforms traditional workflows into efficient, data-driven operations.

At the core of CiriPortal is a comprehensive suite of services that cater to the dynamic needs of both distributors and retailers. We provide real-time inventory tracking, digital product management, sales reporting, order placement, return management, and promotional program tracking. With a clean interface and powerful backend support, users can access all necessary tools to operate smoothly‚Äîwhether they are managing 10 SKUs or 10,000.

One of the standout features of CiriPortal is its flexibility. Distributors can upload and manage their products, set pricing tiers, track retailer activities, and monitor delivery performance. Retailers, on the other hand, gain access to a dedicated dashboard that allows them to view products, place orders, request support, and benefit from targeted offers and loyalty programs.

Our platform also focuses on transparency and communication. We eliminate delays and miscommunication between stakeholders by integrating real-time status updates, push notifications, and automated reports. This allows both distributors and retailers to make informed decisions faster.

CiriPortal Distributor Details

Distributors are the heart of CiriPortal. Our platform provides each distributor with a dedicated dashboard where they can:

  • Upload and manage multiple product categories
  • Assign pricing based on location, retailer type, or volume
  • Track real-time order requests from registered retailers
  • Generate automatic sales reports and performance insights
  • Manage delivery and logistic partners
  • Offer seasonal promotions and discounts
  • Communicate directly with retailers through chat or announcements

Distributors also have access to service and support tools, which allow them to schedule training sessions, manage complaints, and ensure top-tier customer satisfaction. CiriPortal empowers distributors to scale their operations while maintaining full visibility and control over every touchpoint.

CiriPortal Retailer Details

Retailers on CiriPortal gain access to an easy-to-use interface tailored to their business needs. Through their dashboard, retailers can:

  • Browse products from multiple verified distributors
  • Place bulk or item-wise orders with expected delivery timelines
  • Access real-time stock and price availability
  • Participate in loyalty and cashback programs
  • Track their order history, invoice status, and returns
  • Submit queries or requests directly to the distributor
  • Get updated about offers, schemes, and announcements

Retailers benefit from increased transparency, faster deliveries, and access to a wider range of products. Whether you're running a grocery store, pharmacy, or electronics outlet, CiriPortal simplifies your procurement process while offering data insights to support better inventory planning.
