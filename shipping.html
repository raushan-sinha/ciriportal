Shipping & Delivery Policy

🌍 Delivery Locations

  • All cities and towns in India & UAE
  • Urban & semi-urban locations
  • Some remote PIN codes may have limited delivery options

🕒 Delivery Timelines

Product Type Estimated Delivery Time
Foods & Beverages 2–4 working days
Personal Care 3–5 working days
Education Supplies 3–6 working days

Orders placed after 6:00 PM will be processed the next business day.

💸 Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping on all orders above ₹499 / AED 50.
  • Flat fee for orders below the minimum:
    • ₹40 (India)
    • AED 5 (UAE)

📦 Order Processing

  • Orders processed within 24 hours after payment confirmation.
  • Real-time SMS & e-mail updates with tracking details.
  • No shipping on Sundays or public holidays.

🔍 Track Your Order

  1. Visit your Order Dashboard
  2. Use the Track Order button
  3. Contact Customer Support if tracking info isn’t available

🚫 Delivery Delays

Your delivery may be delayed due to:

  • Bad weather
  • Natural disasters
  • Delivery-area restrictions
  • Supplier delays (rare)

We’ll notify you via e-mail/SMS if delays occur.

🛡️ Packaging & Safety

  • Tamper-proof, eco-friendly packaging for all products.
  • Perishables shipped in temperature-controlled, hygienic conditions.
  • Reject damaged/tampered packages and inform us within 12 hours.

❓ Missing or Wrong Items

Contact our Help Desk within 48 hours of delivery. We will investigate and resolve promptly.
